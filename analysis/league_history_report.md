## Key Takeaways for 2026 Draft

1. **Target swing categories**: QS, SVHD, HR — these flip matchup outcomes on thin margins.
2. **Go balanced**: Top finishers maintain competitive win rates across most categories rather than punting.
3. **Draft value cliff at round 13**: Players after this are replacement-level. Keeper value is highest for players who would be drafted in rounds 1-12.
4. **Know your opponents**: Manager tendencies reveal exploitable draft patterns.
